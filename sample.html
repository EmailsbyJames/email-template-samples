NEW YEAR, NEW LOOK FOR #CDW2016
So the festive season is over - and let's be honest we'd all rather be lying in bed eating a bag of sweets, but hey here we are and here's to a fantastic 2016! The year is looking pretty promising for #CDW2016 with more showrooms, more exhibition venues and more design talent than ever before, with a new masterplan by architecture practice OMMX.
Register here now and don't miss the new look CDW!
2016 EXHIBITION VENUES - FROM NORTH TO SOUTH
Offecct
DESIGN FIELDS
New for 2016 is Design Fields, a large-scale curated exhibition of contemporary design, set in a specially constructed two-floor pavilion at Spa Fields. Expect installations, live content and great products!
Baker Street Boys
PLATFORM
Platform returns to the unique underground space House of Detention. See cutting-edge, emerging talent in the cells of this former prison.
Liam Treanor
BRITISH COLLECTION
Another new one - British Collection, will showcase some of the UK's most exciting homegrown talent within the barrel-roofed St James's Crypt.
Hand&Eye Studio
PROJECT
New for #CDW2016, Project presents leading international brands including furniture, lighting and product design in St James's Church garden.
N and N
ADDITIONS
The ever popular Additions, ventures out of the crypt to its new home in St James's Church garden. Visit for interior products and accessories.
Copper & Silk
DETAIL
Detail returns to the majestic Priory Church, Cloister Garden and Pavilion at The Order of St John, hosting brands that reflect the best in luxury craftsmanship and quality.
ICON'S HOUSE OF CULTURE
Resident
Icon's House of Culture returns in a new, unique space - Fabric nightclub. Showcasing an exciting mix of international brands, expect to see this venue in a whole new light.
2016 SHOWROOM UPDATE
New for 2016 is the showroom gateway at Brewhouse Yard. Utilising this central courtyard, visitors will be able to access the huge variety of showrooms taking part in #CDW2016, with hand outs, maps and refreshments on offer. Architects Studio Egret West are developing the look so watch this space…
